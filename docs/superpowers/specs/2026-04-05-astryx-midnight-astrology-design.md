# Astryx Midnight Astrology Design

**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Goal

Shift Astryx away from warm editorial beige and into a premium prediction-product palette that feels mystical, exact, and trustworthy.

## Approved Direction

`Midnight astrology`

- Background uses deep indigo instead of beige or pure black.
- Surfaces use blue-black translucent panels with soft separation.
- Primary text uses moon-cream instead of stark white.
- CTA and small emphasis moments use restrained old-gold.
- Secondary text and lines use misted blue-gray to keep the UI celestial rather than earthy.

## Design Tokens

- `--background: #0d1020`
- `--foreground: #f3ecdf`
- `--surface: rgba(19, 25, 47, 0.76)`
- `--surface-strong: rgba(23, 29, 53, 0.9)`
- `--muted: #9da8c6`
- `--line: rgba(125, 138, 176, 0.22)`
- `--accent: #d8b975`
- `--accent-strong: #7d8ab0`

## Application Rules

- Keep page structure and information architecture unchanged.
- Use gold only for CTA, small badges, and focus moments.
- Avoid purple-heavy mystic styling that makes the app feel cheap.
- Avoid pure black backgrounds that flatten the premium feel.
- Preserve form readability and contrast first, mood second.

## Observatory Layer

Approved enhancement: `Orbital halo`

- Add large, faint orbital rings in the background rather than decorative clutter inside the form.
- Add sparse star points with mixed moon-cream and blue-gray glow.
- Add a restrained outer observatory rim around the form container.
- Keep the form interior clean and operational, not ornate.
- Avoid dense constellations, heavy grid overlays, or brass-heavy antique decoration.

## Scope

This change covers:

- Global color tokens
- Page background gradients
- Surface treatment on the intake form
- Input styling and focus states
- CTA styling

This change does not cover:

- Flow changes
- New pages
- Additional decorative illustrations
